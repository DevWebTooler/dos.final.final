What is dOS?

dOS is a new operating system developed by Damiano Sensi, designed to be intuitive, fast, and modern. With an elegant user interface and optimized performance, dOS offers a smooth user experience on any device. The system will be completely free and available for Windows, Linux, and MacOS, allowing you to transform your current computer into something completely new.
