Frequently Asked Questions

When will dOS be available? +
dOS is currently in development. The private beta will begin in the coming months, followed by a public beta. The official release is planned for the end of next year. Sign up for the beta to be among the first to try it!
Will dOS work on my current computer? +
dOS is designed to work on most modern computers. The minimum requirements will be published before the public beta release. Our goal is to make dOS accessible even on low-end hardware.
Will dOS really be free? +
Yes, dOS will be completely free for personal use. In the future, we may offer an enterprise version with additional features for businesses, but the basic version will always remain free.
How can I contribute to the dOS project? +
There are several ways to contribute: participate in the beta program, report bugs, suggest features, or even contribute to the code through our open-source platform that will be launched soon. Contact us for more information!
